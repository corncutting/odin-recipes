The Finest Caesar Salad

How to prepare the Finest Caesar Salad

This salad is one of the best out there. With this step by step tutorial, you will be able to create a caesar salad that invites the whole family over. Figuratively, the salad unfortunately does not have a phone to invite people over.

Ingredients

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Croutons
  • Caesar Dressing

Steps for Croutons

  1. Preheat the Oven
  2. Cut the Baguettes
  3. Combine 3 TBSP of extra virgin olive oil and 1 TSP of finely minced garlic
  4. Drizzle the Garlic Oil over the Croutons and Sprinkle the top with 2 TBSP grated parmesan cheese
  5. Toss the Mixture and Bake

Steps for Caesar Dressing

  1. Whisk Garlic, Dijon, Worcestershire, Lemon Juice, and Red Wine Vinegar together in a small bowl
  2. Drizzle Extra Virgin Olive Oil in the bowl
  3. Whisk 1/2 TSP Salt and 1/8 TSP Black Pepper

Prepare the Salad

  1. Rinse, Dry, and Chop the Romaine Lettuce
  2. Place in a Large Serving Bowl and Sprinkle with Shredded Parmesan and Croutons
  3. Drizzle with Caesar Dressing until it is evenly coated
  4. Enjoy
